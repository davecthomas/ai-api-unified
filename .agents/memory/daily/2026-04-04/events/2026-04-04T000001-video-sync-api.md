## Why

- All three video providers (OpenAI Sora, Google Veo, Bedrock Nova Reel) use async job patterns but the library's existing contract is synchronous.
- The design keeps the public `AIBaseVideos` interface synchronous with a blocking `generate_video(...)` convenience method and explicit job-control methods.
- Provider job states normalize to a shared `AIVideoGenerationStatus` enum: queued, running, completed, failed, cancelled.

## What changed

- Authored `docs/video_generation_design.md` defining the sync-over-async video API surface.
- Promoted to ADR-0006.
